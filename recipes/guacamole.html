Chunky Guacamole

Description

A super simple recipe for guacamole, a perfect accompaniement for any tex-mex dish (Like our delicious Chicken and Chorizo Fajitas)

Ingredients

  • Avocado - 2 large
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium - diced
  • Coriander - bunch - finely chopped
  • Lime
  • Red onion - small - diced (optional)

Steps

  1. Halve the avocadoes, remove the stones and scoop the flesh into a large bowl
  2. Mash the avocado with the back of a fork, be careful not to mash too much, its much better when left chunky
  3. Add the tomato, coriander and red onion (if using). Mix well to combine together
  4. Cut the lime in half, then both halves over the bowl to add the lime juice
  5. Mix to ensure the lime juice is evenly distributed. Add a pinch of salt if desired.
