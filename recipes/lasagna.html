Lasagna

Description

This is a simple yet delicious recipe for classic italian style lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets - 1 pack
  • Ground beef - 500g
  • Tomato paste - 1 tbsp
  • Tinned tomatoes - 1 x 400g can
  • Fresh Basil - small bunch - finely chopped
  • Large onion - diced
  • Garlic - 2 cloves - crushed
  • Grated mozzarella - 200g
  • Grated parmesan - 100g
  • Olive oil - 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Fry the ground beef in a hot pan with a tablespoon of olive oil
  2. When the ground beef is browned, remove from the pan. Fry the diced onion in the residual beef fats in the pan
  3. When the onions are golden brown, add the crushed garlic and fry for a minute. Then add the browned beef back to the pan and stir well
  4. Add a tablespoon of tomato paste to the pan and stir well, coating the beef and onion. Allow this to fry for 2 minutes
  5. Add the tin of tomatoes and reduce the hob to a low heat. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. After 10 minutes, add the chopped basil and stir
  6. In an oven proof dish, add a small quantity of the meat sauce and coat the bottom of the dish
  7. Add a layer of pasta sheets to cover the dish. Then add a layer of meat sauce and spread evenly to cover the pasta sheets
  8. Repeat these layers until the final layer of meat sauce is added. Top with grated mozzarella and parmesan.
  9. Put the lasagna into a pre-heated oven at 180 Celsius for around 30 minutes (until the cheese is golden and the pasta layers are cooked)
