Chicken Fajitas

Description

A tex-mex twist on classic fajitas, with spiced chicken and chorizo

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast - 500g - diced
  • Old el paso fajita spice mix - 1 sachet
  • Chorizo - 250g - diced
  • Large onion - sliced
  • Red Bell Pepper - sliced
  • Mushrooms - 250g - sliced
  • Vegetable Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Tortilla wraps - 4

Steps

  1. Fry the diced chicken in a hot pan with a tablespoon of vegetable oil
  2. When the chicken is browned, remove from the pan and set aside
  3. Put the pan back onto the heat and add the diced chorizo. Fry for 5 minutes until coloured. Oil is not needed as the chorizo will release its own oils during frying.
  4. Reduce the pan to a medium heat and fry the onions, peppers and mushrooms with the chorizo until softened. Stir frequently.
  5. Add the cooked chicken back to the pan, and add the spice mix. Stir well to coat the chicken, chorizo and vegetables. (add a splash of water if the mix is looking dry)
  6. Warm the tortillas - 20 seconds in the microwave, or 5 minutes in a warm oven (wrapped in foil)
  7. Serve the warm tortillas with the fajita filling mix. For extra flavour, why not add our delicious Chunky Guacamole!
